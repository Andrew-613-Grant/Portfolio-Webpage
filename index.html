about

Andrew began learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript in 2021. His passion for front-end development had been building for numerous years, so when the opportunity arose to begin that journey he jumped on it. Prior to his recent studies, he specialized in cabinetmaking, including design, as well as various multi-media art. He is motivated by his desire to continuously be learning new things and exploring the unknown.

He aspires to put all of his knowledge, from every aspect of his life, towards web development. Andrew is from and currently lives in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.
